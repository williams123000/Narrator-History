The knight was at the castle 
and The old and wise king was a friendly person. 
The knight had a strong affection for the old and wise king. 
One morning, lord of evil went to the castle and abducted the old and wise king. 
The lord of evil took the old and wise king to the Cursed Cave. 
The knight went to talk to the sorcerer to ask for help. 
The sorcerer agreed to provide the information in exchange for a cluster of magic grass than only grows in the sacred lake.
The knight went in search of the plant but the guardian of the lake barred Him from entering.
The knight sang an ancestral song of supplication and the guardian of the lake allowed Him to enter.
The knight took a bunch of magic grass.
The knight returned with the sorcerer. 
The knight handed to the sorcerer the magic grass. 
The sorcerer revealed to the knight the old and wise king's location. 
The sorcerer gave to the knight a spell to put the lord of evil to sleep. 
Wasting no time, the knight went to the Cursed Cave. 
The knight invoked the spell and the lord of evil fell into a deep sleep.
 The knight released the old and wise king.
